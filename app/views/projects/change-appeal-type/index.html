Change appeal type

Appeals sometimes need to change to a new appeal type. This is usually identified at either validation or LPAQ review stage. In most cases the appellant will be asked to resubmit with the correct appeal type, if this is not possible the appeal will be manually added to horizon. The appeal in back office should be closed, with a status to show why. For MVP the case officer will manually email the appelant to arrange this.
