Case management (admin folder)

Case officers store a variety of different docs and files in a Horizon folder called Admin. Scope has been simplified to reflect work done in other areas of the service, and it includes:

  • New 'Case management' accordion element in case detail page
  • Relocation of Case withdrawal and Case history links into Case management
  • Creation of two new pages to add and manage internal correspondence
