Assign members

  • Once the case is ready to start, a manager will assign a case officer to the Case team
  • Once an inspector is identified and allocated to the case, the manager or case officer will assign the inspector to the Case team

Transfer case

  • During the appeal the team might need to transfer the case from one case officer to another, or from inspector to another;
  • This might happen because someone will be off sick, holidays or if someone’s backlog is too big;
